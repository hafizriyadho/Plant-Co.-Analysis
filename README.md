## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Plant Co.'s business performance across **2022–2024** using an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The dashboard enables management to monitor sales quantity, gross profit, and year-over-year performance while identifying underperforming markets, profitable customers, and seasonal sales trends.

Using Power BI, Power Query, and DAX, this solution transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Plant Co. operates across multiple countries and product categories, generating thousands of transactions annually.

Management faces several business challenges:

- Difficulty comparing current performance with previous years
- Limited visibility into country-level performance
- Inability to quickly identify seasonal trends
- Lack of insight into customer profitability
- Manual reporting processes that delay decision-making

To address these issues, an interactive executive dashboard was developed to monitor KPIs, evaluate business performance, and support data-driven decisions.

---

# ❓ Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer several key business questions:

### Executive Performance

- Is the company performing better than last year?
- How much has Quantity increased or decreased?
- Is Gross Profit Margin healthy?

### Sales Performance

- Which months contributed the most growth?
- Which months experienced the largest decline?
- What seasonal patterns exist?

### Geographic Performance

- Which countries generate the lowest sales?
- Which markets require immediate attention?
- Where should marketing investment be focused?

### Product Performance

- Which product categories sell the most?
- Are Indoor, Outdoor, or Landscape products driving growth?
- How does product demand change throughout the year?

### Customer Profitability

- Which customers generate the highest revenue?
- Which customers have low profit margins?
- Which customers should become key accounts?

# 🔍 Key Findings

## 1. Positive Year-over-Year Growth

Plant Co. achieved an increase of **17.05K units** compared to the previous year.

Although overall growth is positive, performance fluctuates throughout the year, indicating seasonality.

---

## 2. Strong Gross Profit Margin

The business maintained a Gross Profit Margin of approximately **39.6%**, suggesting healthy pricing and cost management.

---

## 3. Mid-Year Drives Business Growth

The Waterfall chart indicates that the strongest contribution occurred between **April and July**, while August through November experienced slower growth.

---

## 4. Bottom 10 Countries Need Attention

Countries such as China, France, Sweden, and Greece contribute relatively low YTD performance.

These markets represent opportunities for strategic improvement.

---

## 5. Customer Revenue is Highly Concentrated

The scatter plot shows that a small number of customers generate significantly higher revenue than the majority.

This suggests opportunities for Key Account Management.

---

## 6. Product Demand Shows Seasonal Patterns

Indoor, Outdoor, and Landscape products demonstrate different demand trends throughout the year, indicating opportunities for seasonal inventory planning.

---

# 💼 Business Insights

### Sales

- Overall business continues to grow year-over-year.
- Growth is driven by seasonal demand rather than consistent monthly performance.

### Operations

- Gross Profit remains stable despite fluctuations in quantity.
- Operational efficiency appears well controlled.

### Customers

- Most customers contribute relatively small revenue.
- High-value customers should receive personalized retention strategies.

### Geography

- Several countries consistently underperform.
- Localized marketing campaigns could improve regional performance.

# 🚀 Actionable Recommendations

## 1. Improve Low-Performing Markets

Develop country-specific marketing campaigns for the Bottom 10 countries.

Possible initiatives include:

- Local promotions
- Distributor partnerships
- Product localization

---

## 2. Prepare for Seasonal Demand

Increase inventory before high-demand months and optimize stock during slower periods.

Expected benefits:

- Reduced stockouts
- Lower holding costs
- Improved customer satisfaction

---

## 3. Prioritize High-Value Customers

Segment customers based on revenue and profitability.

Suggested strategy:

- VIP customer programs
- Dedicated account management
- Personalized promotions

---

## 4. Improve Low Gross Profit Customers

Investigate customers with high revenue but low GP%.

Possible actions:

- Review pricing
- Reduce discounts
- Optimize logistics costs

---

## 5. Expand Best-Performing Product Categories

Increase inventory and marketing investment for products showing consistent growth.

---

## 6. Build Predictive Analytics

Future dashboard enhancements should include:

- Sales Forecasting
- Customer Churn Prediction
- Inventory Forecasting

These models will support proactive business planning.
